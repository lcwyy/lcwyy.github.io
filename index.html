Welcome to my research website. My name is Chao Liu, a PhD graduated from Tongji University of China. My research interests focus on application of numerical approaches on geotechnical engineering and geology process in nature. Currently, I am a Lecturer at Guangzhou University, working on coupled CFD-DEM modeling of debris flow. The opensource code OpenFOAM is used for the CFD simulation, together with the code LIGGGHTS for the DEM part. The project CFDEM coupling is utilised for coupling between CFD and DEM.
