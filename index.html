Welcome. This is Geomechanics and Geohazards Research Group (GGRG) at School of Civil Engineering of Guangzhou University. Our research focuses on the development and application of numerical approaches on geotechnical engineering and geology process in nature, such as tunneling, urban excavation, debris flow, and the micromechanical behavior of soils on planet.

Currently, we are working on three topics:
  • The macro-micro mechanism of solid-fluid coupling effect during synchronized grouting of shield tunneling in sandy-cobble strata.
  • Multiscale modeling for the soil responses during the failure process in tunneling (funded by Guangdong-SFC, No. 2018A030313132).
  • In-situ monitoring on the ground responses induced by excavation using distributed fiber-optic sensing.
