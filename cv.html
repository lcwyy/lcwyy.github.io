RESEARCH EXPERIENCES (SELECTED)
  1. 12/2011~12/2013: Design and construction of the GPST shield tunneling method. Funded by: The project was funded by the Shanghai Urban Construction Group. Role: Developed multiple FEM models for predicting the soil responses due to the GPST tunneling process. Simulated tail grouting process during the GPST tunneling process using CFD (via Fluent).
  2. 12/2011~12/2012: Face stability analysis for tunneling in the strata composed of cobble and sand. Funded by: Beijing MTR Construction Administration Corporation. Role: Conducted the numerical simulation framework for the face stability analysis. Carried out the DEM analysis for the triaxial compression test of cobbles and the tunneling process, using the DEM code Ellip3d and paraEllip3d.
  3. 06/2010~01/2014: Research and development of the key technology for tunnel face stability and risk control of tunneling under Qiantang River. Funded by: The Ministry of Transport of P.R. China. Role: acted as the chief participant using DEM and FEM to study the failure modes of tunnel face in sandy soil, with the consideration of pore pressure caused by tidal bores in Qiantang River.
  4. 04/2010~05/2010: Feasibility study of the construction of large shield tunnel across the Qiongzhou Strait. Funded by: The Ministry of Railways of P.R. China. Role: acted as the chief researcher in charge of the numerical analysis of tunnel face stability.
  5. 02/2010~05/2013: Research of multiple disturbing effects on pile groups caused by the multi-crossing tunneling of a super-diameter slurry shield (15.43m). Funded by: Shanghai Tunnel Engineering Co., Ltd.. Role: acted as the independent numerical and theoretical analyst.
  6. 11/2009~01/2010: Feasibility study of constructing the framework of Huashan Road Subway Station using a novel curved pipe jacking technology. Funded by: Shanghai Urban Construction Group. Role: acted as the independent numerical and theoretical analyst (via FEM on ABAQUS platform).
  7. 05/2009~10/2009: Research and development of key technology for constructing the grade separation system “East-West Transportation System of Shanghai”. Funded by: Shanghai Foundation Engineering Co., Ltd.. Role: acted as the project director and independent numerical analyst (via FEM on ABAQUS platform).
  8. 12/2008~04/2009: Experimental research of reinforced soil surrounding shield departure and reception shafts using a true triaxial test system. Funded by: Shanghai Tunnel Engineering Co., Ltd.. Role: acted as the project director and chief laboratory assistant studying the mechanical properties of cemented soil and deciphering failure modes in the model test.
